History of Computers

We use computers every day. The first electronic computers were invented in the 1940s. The first computer was designed in the 1800s, it was mechanical not electronic. One of the first computers was an abacus, invented in 500 B.C. Computers process things in binary. In 1833 Charles Babbage invented all the parts a modern computer uses. In 1946 scientists at the University of Pennsyivania built ENIAC, considered the first super computer. Programmers give Artificial Intelligence an algorithm. It is important to know all about computers.
